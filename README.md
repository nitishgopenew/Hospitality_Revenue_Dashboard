# 🏨 Hospitality Revenue Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes hotel performance data to uncover insights related to revenue, occupancy, and booking trends. The dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions to improve revenue and operational efficiency.

The analysis is based on a real-world business scenario where a hotel chain faced declining revenue due to ineffective decision-making and rising competition.

## 🎯 Business Objective

* Identify factors impacting revenue performance
* Analyze occupancy trends across cities and properties
* Evaluate booking platform contributions
* Enable data-driven decision-making

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **Revenue**
* **RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room)**
* **ADR (Average Daily Rate)**
* **Occupancy %**
* **Realisation %**

## 📊 Key Insights

* Weekend occupancy is significantly higher than weekdays
* Direct offline bookings generate higher ADR
* Certain properties outperform others in revenue and ratings
* Booking platforms contribute differently to overall revenue

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus on high-performing properties to maximize revenue
* Improve weekday occupancy through targeted promotions
* Optimize pricing strategies across booking platforms
* Strengthen direct booking channels for higher profitability
